**Definition:**

*   **Collateral:** They are NOT backed by taxpayer labor (income tax). They are backed specifically by the **future revenue streams and asset sales** of the Sovereign Humanity Fund.

**The Mechanics:**
1.  **Issuance:** The Treasury issues $2 Trillion in ADBs in Years 1-5.

4.  **Repayment:** In Year 20+, as the SHF collects massive dividends from mature AI giants (or sells small tranches of appreciated stock), it retires the bonds.

**Why Investors Buy It:**
It is a "Bet on Automation." If you believe AI will create wealth, this bond is AAA-rated. It is an index fund of the entire AI future, wrapped in a government guarantee.

Critics ask: *"Won't printing $2 Trillion cause hyper-inflation?"*

**The Deflationary Counter-Force:**
Usually, printing money chases *scarce goods* = Inflation.
But AI is a massive **Deflationary Force**. It drives the cost of goods/services to near zero.
*   **The Macro Balance:**
    *   AI pushes prices DOWN (Deflation).
    *   ADBs push money supply UP (Inflation).
    *   **Target:** Price Stability (0-2% inflation).
    *   We *need* to print money to counteract the deflationary collapse of prices, or debt becomes unserviceable.
