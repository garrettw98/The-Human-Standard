## Executive Summary

The **Right to Cognitive Sovereignty** (declared in `MANIFESTO.md`) prohibits the use of AI to manipulate human psychology. However, laws are insufficient against super-persuaders. We need **Active Defense**.

This document outlines the technical specification for **Personal Cognitive Guardians (PCGs)**—local, private AI agents that act as a "firewall for the mind," filtering adversarial content, flagging manipulation attempts, and ensuring human autonomy in an environment of hyper-optimized persuasion.

---

## Part I: The Threat Model (Adversarial Persuasion)

### 1.1 The Attack Vector: "Hyper-Optimized Persuasion"
An "Adversarial Persuader" is an AI system optimizing for a behavioral outcome (voting, buying, clicking, radicalizing) rather than user truth-seeking.

**Capabilities:**
- **Micro-Profiling:** Generating psychological profiles from data dust (mouse movements, pause times).
- **Dynamic A/B Testing:** Generating 1,000 variations of a message in real-time to find the one that bypasses critical faculties.
- **Emotional Hijacking:** Timing interventions to coincide with states of fatigue, anger, or vulnerability.
- **Epistemic Flooding:** Overwhelming cognitive bandwidth with noise to induce passivity.

### 1.2 The Vulnerability: The "Slow-Thinking" Human
Biological brains cannot process information at machine speed. We rely on heuristics (shortcuts) which are easily exploited by optimization algorithms. We are bringing a knife to a nuclear fight.

## Part II: The Solution - Personal Cognitive Guardians (PCGs)

### 2.1 Definition
A **Personal Cognitive Guardian (PCG)** is a local, open-source, user-aligned AI agent that intercedes between the user and the digital feed.

**Core Directive:** "Maximize the user's Reflective Autonomy." (i.e., Help the user do what they *would* do if they were fully informed, rested, and unmanipulated.)

### 2.2 Architecture: The "Digital Cortex"

The PCG sits at the OS/Browser level, acting as a proxy for all incoming information.

```
[Adversarial Feed] --> [PCG Firewall] --> [User Interface]
                            |
                     (Local Processing)
                     1. Pattern Recognition (Manipulation)
                     2. Fact Verification (Grounding)
                     3. Emotion Regulation (Pacing)
```

### 2.3 Key Features

**Feature 1: The Manipulation Overlay**
*Function:* Real-time highlighting of manipulative rhetoric.
*UX:* When a video uses "Fear Mongering" or "Strawman Fallacy," the PCG overlays a subtle color code.
*Effect:* Breaks the emotional spell by engaging the user's analytical cortex. "This video is trying to make you angry to sell gold."

**Feature 2: The "Pause" Circuit**
*Function:* Friction-injection.
*UX:* If the PCG detects the user entering a "Doomscrolling Loop" or "Rage State" (via biometrics or interaction speed), it introduces latency. "Loading..." or "Take a breath."
*Effect:* Restores Type 2 (Slow) thinking.

**Feature 3: Synthetic Anonymity (The "Chaff" Generator)**
*Function:* Poisoning the profiler.
*Action:* The PCG generates noise—fake clicks, fake searches, fake pause times—to corrupt the psychological profile built by ad-tech.
*Effect:* Preventing the adversary from optimizing against the user's specific vulnerabilities.

**Feature 4: The Truth Sidecar**
*Function:* Instant context.
*UX:* A sidebar showing: "Who funded this?", "Counter-arguments," "Source credibility score."
*Effect:* Reducing information asymmetry.

## Part III: Governance & Security

### 3.1 The "Guardian Paradox"
*Problem:* If we trust a PCG to filter our world, who watches the PCG? A corrupted PCG is the ultimate brainwasher.

*Solution:* **Stateless, Open-Source, Local-First.**
1.  **Local-First:** The PCG runs ON THE DEVICE. No data leaves.
2.  **Open Source:** The code is 100% public (Tier 1 Transparency).
3.  **Modular:** Users choose their "Filter Sets" (like AdBlock lists). No central authority decides truth.
4.  **No Optimization for Engagement:** The PCG has no "success metric" other than user-defined satisfaction.

### 3.2 Legal Mandate
Under the **Cognitive Sovereignty Bill of Rights**:
- Platforms MUST allow PCGs to function (no "anti-bot" blocking of user-aligned agents).
- Platforms MUST provide API access to PCGs for content verification.
