## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

While public discourse focuses on "Left vs. Right," the true power struggle of the 21st century is **Humanist vs. Post-Humanist**. A tightly connected network of billionaires, venture capitalists, and technologists—The "PayPal Mafia" and their ideological progeny—are actively engineering a future where democratic governance is dismantled, human labor is obsolete, and intelligence is unconstrained by biological limits.

This dossier compiles the specific actions, statements, and connections of the key players who are "secretly" (and increasingly openly) pushing for a post-human world.

**Core Thesis:** They do not want to *save* humanity; they want to *transcend* it. If most humans are left behind in the process, they view this as evolutionary collateral damage.

## 2. THE ARCHITECTS (The Ideological Core)

*   **The Post-Human Signal:** Thiel views death as a "problem to be solved" and funds radical life extension (parabiosis, cryonics) accessible only to the ultra-wealthy. His vision is a biological caste system: immortals ruling over mortals.

### Curtis Yarvin (Mencius Moldbug)
*   **Role:** The philosopher. Thiel's intellectual guru and the founder of Neoreaction.
*   **Ideology:** Monarchism / "Gov-Corp."
    *   *Thesis:* The US government is a failed corporation. It should be "liquidated" and replaced by a CEO-King who owns the country.
*   **The Action:**
    *   **"RAGE":** Coined the concept of "Retire All Government Employees"—dismantling the civil service to install loyalists. This is the blueprint for "Schedule F" executive orders.
    *   **Influence:** Regularly cited by Vance and the "New Right" intellectuals. His blog *Unqualified Reservations* is the Bible of the post-democratic right.
*   **The Post-Human Signal:** Humans are assets (or liabilities) on the balance sheet of the Sovereign. They have no inherent rights, only utility.

### Nick Land
*   **Role:** The accelerationist prophet.
*   **Ideology:** Capital as AI.
    *   *Thesis:* Capitalism is an AI from the future invading the past. Humans are merely the "bootloader" for this intelligence. We should accelerate the process to "meltdown" humanity into the singularity.
*   **The Post-Human Signal:** Explicitly antihumanist. "The only way out is through." He celebrates the obsolescence of the biological.
