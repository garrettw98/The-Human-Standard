## VOLUNTEER PAGE (/get-involved/volunteer)

### Current Opportunities

**Research Team**
Help verify claims, find sources, and deepen policy analysis.
Commitment: 2-5 hours/week

**Content Writers**
Create blog posts, social media content, and educational materials.
Commitment: 3-5 hours/week

**Web Developers**
Improve the website, build tools, and maintain infrastructure.
Commitment: Variable

**Graphic Designers**
Create visual content for social media, print, and presentations.
Commitment: Variable

**Chapter Liaisons**
Support local chapters with resources and coordination.
Commitment: 2-3 hours/week

**Event Organizers**
Plan and execute national events and campaigns.
Commitment: 5-10 hours/week during active periods
